HOW TO ADD MANUALLY (Chrome/Edge):
  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+B to show bookmarks bar
  2. Right-click the bar → "Add page..." or "Add bookmark"
  3. Name: Grab Page Text
  4. URL: paste the javascript: line above
  5. Save

HOW TO USE:
  - On any normal page: click it once → auto-copies all text
  - If text is selected already: copies just your selection
  - On pages with iframe readers (Pearson, McGraw-Hill, etc.):
      a popup appears → Ctrl+A, Ctrl+C inside the reader → Ctrl+V in the popup box → done
